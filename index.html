Stellar Craft. Genuine Care.

At Stella Nails, the name says it all. Stella means star — and that's exactly the standard we hold ourselves to. Nestled on Bloomfield Avenue in the heart of Caldwell, we've built a reputation as the go-to destination for residents who refuse to settle for anything less than brilliant.
